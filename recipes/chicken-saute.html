Chicken sauté

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 300 g chicken breast (cut into cubes)
  • 1 onion
  • 1 green pepper
  • 1/2 red pepper
  • 1 chopped tomato
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 1 teaspoon dried mint

Steps

  1. Add 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil to a pan. Add 300 g diced chicken breast and sauté on high heat.
  2. Add 1 chopped onion to the cooked chicken and continue sautéing.
  3. Add 1 chopped green pepper and 1/2 chopped kapya (red) pepper.
  4. Add 1 chopped tomato to the pan.
  5. After some of the liquid evaporates, add 1 teaspoon each of salt, black pepper, red pepper flakes, and dried mint. Mix well.
  6. Once fully cooked, remove from heat and serve. Chicken sauté is ready. Enjoy!
