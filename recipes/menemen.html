Menemen

Description

A classic Turkish breakfast dish made with eggs cooked in a mixture of tomatoes, peppers, onions, and olive oil.

Ingredients

  • 4 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1/2 onion
  • 4 green chili peppers
  • 1 kapya pepper (red pepper)
  • 4 tomatoes
  • 1 stalk green onion
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper

Steps

  1. Add olive oil to a non-stick pan and sauté the finely chopped onion until lightly golden.
  2. Add the green chili peppers and kapya (red) peppers, then continue sautéing.
  3. When the peppers start to soften, add the diced tomatoes.
  4. Cover the pan and cook until the tomatoes soften completely.
  5. This will take about 10 minutes.
  6. Add the finely chopped green onion.
  7. When the onions soften, crack in the eggs, add the spices, and mix.
  8. Stir until the eggs are cooked, then turn off the heat and serve the onion menemen hot.
  9. Enjoy!
